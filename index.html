About

Who I Am

I am interested in Graph Theory, specifically the existence and uniqueness of Hamiltonian cycles.

I've taught mathematics at the International School of Prague, lectured at the Czech MENSA Summer Camp, and tutored university and high school students since 2018.

Exploring Sheehan's Conjecture: do uniquely Hamiltonian regular graphs exist? Extending Fleischner's construction via the Blanuša snark.

Beyond research, I engage in creative writing and music.
